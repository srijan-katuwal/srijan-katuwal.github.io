Our Offering

Our Offering

Our BDE program ensures the mandatory:

  • 20 hours online course
  • 10 hours of digital course
  • 10 hours of in-car one on one lessons

Our Beginner Driver Education Programs is affordable to the people of all ages with an easy payment plan.

Our in class and in car instructors are certified instructors by the Ministry of Transportation. They are highly experienced, professional, patient and dedicated to creating a conducive learning environment. Google Reviews of our students trained by ZDS speak of professionalism of our staff.

Our online/digital courses are designed to be interesting and are equipped with all the necessary advanced education tools and videos. Our aim is to enable a fun learning experience for our students which does not become monotonous.

Driving courses are available all year round. After registration, we offer students to pick any time of the day and go through a digital program. You may postpone your training if you have other commitments with work or school. If you are unable to attend a session after registration, make-up sessions are an option within a period of one year for digital learning as well as the in- car lessons.

Techniques: ZAB Driving School classes are designed for you to pass your driving test displaying safe and expert driving skills with a strong focus on defensives driving techniques. At ZAB Driving School, we teach emergency maneuvers and techniques to prevent the consequences of distracted driving. We aim to inculcate safe driving habits and eventually hope to help the Ministry reduce the overall road-accident occurrences in Ontario.

G1 license holders may be able to qualify for a 4-month reduction in the 12-month minimum G1- licensing period. After passing G2 road test, students qualified from ZAB Driving School may also benefit from a reduction in insurance premiums.

Our website also provides you free course on how to pass your G1 written test and there another paid course on how to pass your G2 road test.
