Frequently Asked Questions

  • Q.1 Does your school conduct ministry approved courses?

    Our school is ministry approved therefore we conduct beginner Drivers Education course which is under the guidelines and supervision of MTO.

  • Q.2 What all do you provide in a BDE course?

    We provide 30 hours of online self study course material including videos and assignments and 10 hours of in car one on one lessons.

  • Q.3 Can I start the course before the age of 16?

    Driving course can be undertaken any time but in car lessons can only be taken after you acquire G1 license. Age requirement for G1 is 16 years.

  • Q.4 Do you provide insurance certificate after qualifying the course?

    If you take a course at any ministry approved driving school like ours then you qualify for the insurance certificate. This certificate is issued by ministry through Service Ontario after you pass your G2.

  • Q.5 How much time do you provide to complete the course? How much time is required to complete the course on fast track?

    We give one year to complete the course however if someone wants to finish it fast, we will help you to do that. You also have to be available to get scheduled any time in coordination with your in car instructor. You are also required to finish 30 hours online course in your own time at a faster pace.

  • Q.6 Do I have to be available for online course with instructor?

    No, it is fully independent study, in your own place, at your own pace. All time will be recorded. You don’t have to be available before instructor however he is observing all your self study at the background.

  • Q.7 Can I also get the certificate if I just do few in car lessons?

    No, you qualify for certificate only after you fulfil requirements mentioned in Q 2.

  • Q.8 How do I start if I am ready to undergo a course at your school?

    Open our website www.zabdrivingschool.com

    Click on Registration tab and submit after filling in all details. Get in touch with us for payment and we send you the link to start online course. Side by side you can get in touch with us to get your in-car lessons scheduled.

  • Q.9 Will I be using my own car for in car lessons?

    Car is not your problem. Your driving instructor will bring his car which is equipped with extra brake for the instructor for safety.

  • Q.10 Which payment options do you accept, and do you have any easy payment plan?

    You can pay through your debit/credit card online through our website, after you Register. Or through Interac Etransfer on (oakville@zabdrivingschool.com) or through machine available with our driving instructor (only after coordinating with him). Yes, we do offer payment through 2- 3 easy installments.

  • Q.11 Who will book my road test? Is road test fee also included in the BDE course fee?

    You can book road test on Drive Test website www.drivetest.ca

    Your driving instructor can help you booking but the road test booking or use of instructors car for road test is not included in BDE course fee. You may read the Car Rental Fee for test on our Pricing Tab of our website.

  • Q.12 Do I have any other benefit of BDE course, other than Insurance Certifcate?

    In normal circumstances, after obtaining G1 license, you can appear in road test after one year. If you undertake BDE course, you may qualify to appear in road test after 8 months.

  • Q.13 I am a new immigrant. I also have background experience of driving. Do I have to undertake BDE course?

    You don’t have to undertake BDE course if your experience was added at the time of enrollment for G1 License. You don’t have to wait for one year or 8 months to appear in road test. BDE course, however may offer you Insurance discount which is not possible even if you had background experience. For getting the benefit of insurance, it is recommended to undertake BDE course.
